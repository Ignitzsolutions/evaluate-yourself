# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Job-seeking students and professionals preparing for interviews, plus admins/mentors monitoring candidate quality and platform operations. Candidates primarily use the product in focused prep sessions before interviews; admins use it in dashboard workflows to monitor activity, reliability, security, and coaching outcomes.

## Product Purpose
Evaluate Yourself helps people improve interview communication through guided practice, realtime/session-based evaluation, and actionable feedback reports. It exists to make interview readiness measurable and improvable with repeatable drills, analytics, and coaching signals. Success means users can practice consistently, understand weak areas quickly, and improve confidence and clarity over time.

## Brand Personality
Confident, practical, and supportive. The tone should feel trustworthy and coaching-oriented rather than flashy: clear instruction, low friction, and focused feedback that reduces anxiety before high-stakes interviews.

## Anti-references
- Over-gamified learning UIs that prioritize badges/novelty over skill transfer.
- Hyper-corporate sterile dashboards that feel cold and intimidating for learners.
- Visual noise patterns: excessive gradients, decorative motion, and trendy effects that distract from practice flow.

## Design Principles
1. Keep users in the practice loop: prompt, perform, feedback, next action.
2. Prioritize clarity over decoration for all task-critical surfaces.
3. Make progress visible with concrete, interpretable signals.
4. Reduce cognitive load at high-stakes moments (login, voice setup, review).
5. Preserve consistency between candidate and admin experiences while matching each role’s job-to-be-done.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Target WCAG 2.1 AA across candidate and admin surfaces. Maintain readable contrast, clear focus states, keyboard-accessible primary flows, reduced-motion-safe interactions, and plain-language feedback/error copy for first-time users and non-native English speakers.
